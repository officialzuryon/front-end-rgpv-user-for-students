FUNCTIONAL COMMUNICATION - II

Maximum Marks : 70

Note :

Section - A (Oral Skills)
1.
Handling calls, telephone manners etc. can be evaluated through the role plays (in pairs) : (Any one) 15
a) Handling an irate customer
b) Making a hotel reservation
c) Cancelling an already booked package
2.
Group discussion can be conducted on any one of the following topics : 20
a) Tourism industry in India
b) "Atithi Devo Bhav"
c) Hospitality industry
