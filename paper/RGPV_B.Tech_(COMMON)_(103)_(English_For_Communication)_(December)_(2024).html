English For Communication

Course: B.Tech Code: 103 Branch: COMMON Date: December 2024

Save as PDF

Opens your browser print dialog — select "Save as PDF" to download.

Total No. of Questions : 8 Total No. of Printed Pages : 3
Roll No

BT-103 (GS)

B.Tech., I & II Semester

Examination, December 2024

Grading System (GS)

English for Communication

Time: Three Hours Maximum Marks : 70

Note: i) Attempt any five questions.

ii) All questions carry equal marks.

1.
a) Change the below sentences from Passive Voice to Active Voice. 7
  • i) The mailman was barked at by the dog.
  • ii) The table was jumped on by the cat.
  • iii) The chef is preparing a delicious meal.
  • iv) The essay was written by James.
  • v) The kennel was cleaned by the attendant.
  • vi) The movie was directed by Christopher Nolan.
  • vii) The chemistry book was read by the students.
b) Select the verb tense that corresponds with the subject correctly. 7
  • i) She ____ (goes/went) to the store every morning.
  • ii) They ____ (is/are) studying for their exams right now?
  • iii) Neither Joseph nor his family ____ French. (speaks/speak)
  • iv) The cat ____ (sleeps/slept) peacefully on the windowsill.
  • v) They ____ (will/have) completed the project by next week.
  • vi) He ____ (will/had) finish the task before the deadline.
  • vii) The birds ____ (chirp/chirped) loudly in the morning.
2.
a) Add the correct prefix or suffix to the word in parenthesis to complete the sentences. 7
  • i) If the children ____ (behave), they must go to bed early.
  • ii) Dogs are great for those looking for ____ (companion).
  • iii) To me, there is nothing worse than ____ (bore).
  • iv) Rocky is the most ____ (grace) dancer in the group.
  • v) The lesson is full of mistakes, we will have to ____ (write) it.
  • vi) His support gives the case ____ (legitimate).
  • vii) You can't ____ (do) what's already been done.
b) Write the antonyms of the following words. 7

Enormous, Exodus, Mortal, Ignorant, Occupied, Abounds, Feasibility.

3.
a) Describe the benefits and drawbacks of written communication. 7
b) Explain the communication cycle and process. What makes one-way and two-way communication different from each other? 7
4.
a) What is planning, drafting and editing? Explain in details. 7
b) What is the introduction of a lab report? How to write a lab report? 7
5.
a) Give difference between verbal and non-verbal communication. 7
b) What are Tender issues? Why is tender needed? Give answer. 7
6.
a) Why reading comprehension is important? How to improve comprehension? 7
b) What is the most common sentence structure? What are the elements of a sentence? Write. 7
7.
a) How do you write a trouble report? Explain the structure of a report and its purpose. 7
b) You went for a vacation with your family to Dehradun and stayed in hotel Ashoka & Co. However, you are not satisfied with their service. Write a letter describing your stay and providing relevant suggestions. You are Vivan/ Priyal Joshi. 7
8
Answer the following. (any two) 2×7=14
  • a) Precise writing
  • b) Guidelines for writing Resume
  • c) Calling Quotation
  • d) Technical Description
