COMMUNICATION SKILL IN ENGLISH

Time : Three Hours

Maximum Marks : 70

Note : Attempt all questions.

  1. 1.
    a) Choose the correct answer from the given alternatives: 2×1=2
    1. Communication using signs, gestures, or body language is called:
      1. Verbal communication
      2. Written communication
      3. Non-verbal communication
      4. Oral communication
    2. Which one is part of body language?
      1. Email
      2. Hand movement
      3. Radio message
      4. Written note
    b) Answer the following questions in short (any two) 2×3=6
    1. Explain the process of communication with a diagram.
    2. What is Grapevine Communication? Give an example.
    3. What are the Seven C's of communication? List them.
    c) Answer in detail. 1×6=6
    1. Explain physical barriers in communication.

      OR

      Explain psychological barriers in communication.

  2. 2.
    a) Choose the correct answer from the given alternatives: 2×1=2
    1. Assertiveness is a type of:
      1. Soft skill
      2. Hard skill
      3. Local skill
      4. Communication barrier
    2. Emotional Intelligence includes:
      1. Stress
      2. Resilience
      3. Confusion
      4. Anxiety
    b) Attempt any two of the following questions: (word limit 40-50 words for each) 2×2=4
    1. What is Empathy?
    2. Define Conflict Management.
    3. Why is Time Management important in professional life?
    c) Answer in detail. 1×6=6

    Discuss any three Life Skills.

    OR

    Explain how motivation helps in achieving goals.

  3. 3.
    a) Choose the correct answer from the given alternatives: 2×1=2
    1. Who wrote 'An Astrologer's Day'?
      1. O. Henry
      2. R.K. Narayan
      3. Robert Frost
      4. Jerome K. Jerome
    2. In the poem *Stopping by Woods on a Snowy Evening*, what stops the horse?
      1. A storm
      2. The dark forest
      3. The frozen lake
      4. The village road
    b) Answer the following questions in short (one from each section) 2×2=4
    1. Why did the astrologer leave his village in *An Astrologer's Day*?

      OR

      What gift did Della give to her husband in *The Gift of the Magi*?

    2. How long did the mother suffer after the sting in *Night of the Scorpion*?

      OR

      Who is the speaker in *Where the Mind is Without Fear*?

    c) Answer in detail. 2×5=10
    1. Write a short summary of *Stopping by Woods on a Snowy Evening*.

      OR

      What is the theme of Nissim Ezekiel's poem *Night of the Scorpion*?

    2. How does the astrologer fool people with his clever talk?

      OR

      Describe how Uncle Podger creates confusion while hanging the picture.

  4. 4.
    a) Choose the correct answer from the given alternatives: 2×1=2
    1. What is the correct way to start a letter to a company?
      1. Dear Sir/Madam
      2. Hi there!
      3. To whomever you are
      4. Greetings!
    2. What should you do before sending an email?
      1. Send it immediately
      2. Proofread for mistakes
      3. Ignore it
      4. Delete it
    b) Answer the following questions in short. 1×2=2

    What is the correct format for the date in a formal letter? Give example.

    OR

    Write a brief email to your supervisor requesting a day off due to sickness.

    c) Read the given unseen passage. 1×4=4

    Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. They are known for their intelligence, strong social bonds, and memory. Elephants live in groups called herds, usually led by the oldest female, known as the matriarch. They communicate using sounds, body language, and even vibrations through the ground. Elephants play a crucial role in their ecosystems by helping to maintain the environment. For example, they create water holes by other animals and help spread seeds by eating fruits and excreting the seeds in different locations. Unfortunately, elephant populations are declining due to habitat loss and poaching. Conservation efforts are essential to protect these magnificent creatures and ensure their survival for future generations.

    1. Make a précis of the above given passage in about one-third of its length and give a suitable title.

      OR

    2. Read the above passage and answer these multiple choice questions:
      1. What is a group of elephants called?
        1. Pack
        2. Flock
        3. Herd
        4. Swarm
      2. Who usually leads an elephant herd?
        1. The youngest male
        2. The oldest women
        3. The strongest male
        4. The largest elephant
      3. Why are elephant populations declining?
        1. Over population
        2. Climate change
        3. Habitat loss and poaching
        4. Lack of food
      4. Provide a suitable title for the above passage.
    d) Draft a letter placing an order for 100 office chairs. 1×6=6

    OR

    Draft a job application letter for the post of Assistant Engineer.

  5. 5.
    A) Match the following. 4×1=4
    Column-A Column-B
    i) A person who delays task a) Punctuality
    ii) A person who leads a team b) Procrastinator
    iii) Skill of solving problems c) Leader
    iv) Ability to manage time d) Problem solving
    B) Fill in the blanks with suitable determiners: 2×1=2
    1. There aren't _____ apples left in the basket. (many/much)
    2. _____ the boys are waiting outside. (All/Every)
    b) Rewrite the following choosing the correct verb in each case from the parenthesis. 2×1=2
    1. Neither of the students _____ arrived yet. (has/have)
    2. The train _____ at 5 PM sharp. (arrive/arrives)
    c) Fill in the blanks with simple or continuous form (present tense) of the verb given in the parenthesis: 2×1=2
    1. He _____ his project report right now (write)
    2. The teacher _____ the class every morning. (conduct)
    d) Change into passive voice: 2×1=2
    1. They will deliver the parcel tomorrow.
    2. The manager approved the proposal.
    e) Insert appropriate preposition. 2×1=2
    1. This dining set is made _____ stainless steel. (of/off)
    2. The Second World War ended _____ 1945. (in/on)
