First Year Pharmacy
HUMAN ANATOMY AND PHYSIOLOGY
(ER20-14T)
Time : Three Hours
Maximum Marks : 80
Note : i) Attempt any five questions.
ii) All questions carry equal marks.
iii) Draw neat and labelled diagram wherever necessary.
  1. 1.
    Attempt any two.
    a) Describe myopathy and cramps.
    b) Classify tissues with a labelled diagram.
    c) Write a note on Gall-Bladder.
  2. 2.
    Attempt any two.
    a) Give an account of menstrual cycle.
    b) Enlist the name of endocrine glands and describe the disorders of thyroid gland.
    c) Explain:
    1. i) Glaucoma
    2. ii) Keratitis
  3. 3.
    Write a note on: (Any four)
    a) Hypothyroidism
    b) Myopia
    c) Hypermetropia
    d) Cardiac output
    e) Dyspnea
  4. 4.
    Attempt any two.
    a) Differentiate between sympathetic and parasympathetic nervous system.
    b) Describe the mechanism of blood coagulation.
    c) Explain:
    1. i) Marasmus
    2. ii) Night Blindness
  5. 5.
    Explain. (Any four)
    a) Draw a neat and labelled diagram of nephron.
    b) Draw a neat and labelled diagram of Animal cell.
    c) Write the functions of lymphatic system.
    d) Draw a well labelled diagram of neuron cell.
    e) Write the functions of gastric juices.
  6. 6.
    Write short note on any four.
    a) Functions of retina
    b) Functions of liver
    c) Osteoarthritis
    d) Functions of Human heart
    e) Draw a neat and labelled L.S. diagram of stomach.
  7. 7.
    Attempt any two.
    a) Discuss the physiology of muscle contraction and relaxation.
    b) Differentiate between the following: (Any two)
    1. i) Sensory nerves and Motor nerves.
    2. ii) Smooth muscles and Cardiac muscles.
    3. iii) Compact bone and Spongy bone.
    c) Draw a labelled diagram of internal structure of heart.
  8. 8.
    Attempt any two.
    a) Explain:
    1. i) Rickets
    2. ii) Balanced diet
    3. iii) Bronchitis
    4. iv) Gouts
    b) Name the synovial joints and fixed joints.
    c) Explain the process of carbohydrate metabolism in liver.
