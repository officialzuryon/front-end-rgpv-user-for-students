PHARMACOTHERAPEUTICS
(ER20-24T)
Time : Three Hours
Maximum Marks : 80
Note :
  1. Attempt any five questions out of eight.
  2. Each question carry equal marks.

a) Define Evidence Based Medicines. Write the steps involved in Evidence Based Medicines.
8
b) Explain Standard Treatment Guidelines (STG). Write the advantages of STG to patients and Health Care Workers.
