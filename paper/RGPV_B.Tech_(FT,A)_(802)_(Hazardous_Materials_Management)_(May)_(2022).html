The time required for the center of the cloud to reach the residential area. Assume a wind speed of 2 m/s.

b)

The maximum concentration of chlorine in the residential area. Compare this with a TLV for chlorine of 0.5 ppm. What stability conditions and wind speed produces the maximum concentration?

c)

Determine the distance the cloud must travel to disperse the cloud to a maximum concentration below the TLV. Use the conditions of Part b.
