Opto-Electronics Integrated Circuits

Course: M.Tech Code: 301 Branch: MEVD Date: November 2023

MEVD-301(A)

M.E./M.Tech. III Semester

Examination, November 2023

Opto-Electronics Integrated Circuits

(Elective - I)

Time : Three Hours

Maximum Marks : 70

Note: i) Attempt any five questions.

ii) All questions carry equal marks.

1. a)
Describe principle and working of optical one-dimensional planar waveguide.
b)
Describe waveguide modes and its cutoff conditions.
2. a)
Derive and explain transcendental equations for two dimensional waveguides.
b)
For an optical waveguide explain surface scattering, absorption losses, radiation and bending losses.
3. a)
Describe fabrication process for optical waveguides. Write all the steps and explain with help of suitable diagram.
b)
Write down difference between vacuum-deposition and solution-deposition during waveguide fabrication techniques?
4. a)
Explain about epitaxial growth of III-V compound semiconductor materials.
b)
Differentiate and explain ion-exchange and ion implanted wave guides.
5. a)
Explain the procedure of coupling between optical waveguides.
b)
Describe the working of directional couplers and also give its applications.
6. a)
Explain the working principles of waveguide modulator and switches.
b)
Describe the electro-optic, acousto-optic and magneto-optic effects used in light modulators.
7 a)
Define the quantum efficiency and the responsivity of a photo detector. Derive an expression for the responsivity of an intrinsic photo detector. Derive the wave length at which the quantum efficiency and the responsivity are equal.
b)
Explain the working principle of distributed feedback laser.
8.
Write short notes on any two of the following:
a)
Shaping of waveguides by wet and dry etching techniques
b)
Prism couplers and Grating couplers
c)
Tunable laser diodes and optical integrated circuits
