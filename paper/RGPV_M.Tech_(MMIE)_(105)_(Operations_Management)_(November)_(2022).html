MMIE-105

M.E./M.Tech., I Semester

Examination, November 2022

Operations Management

Time : Three Hours Maximum Marks : 70

Note: i) Attempt any five questions.

ii) All questions carry equal marks.

1. a)
What do you understand by operations management? Describe the different functions of operations management.
b)
What are production systems? Explain the different types of production systems.
2. a)
Define plant layout. Elaborate the different types of plant layout.
b)
What is production planning and control? Discuss the functions of production planning and control.
3.
What do you mean by aggregate plan and master production scheduling? Describe how the aggregate plans and master production schedules serve as initiators of action in other functional activities of the organization.
4. a)
What is maintenance management? Elaborate the different types of maintenance management strategies.
b)
Differentiate between individual and group replacement policies.
5. a)
What is material management? Explain the objectives and planning of material management.
b)
Critically examine the EOQ model of inventory.
6. a)
What is line balancing? Explain the methods of line balancing.
b)
Write short note on evolution of operations management.
7. a)
"Breakdown maintenance is maintenance performed on equipment that has broken down and is unusable". Explain.
b)
Explain product life cycle and why is it necessary to launch a new product.
8.
Write short notes on any two of the following:
  1. Overview of MRP and MRP-II
  2. JIT system of manufacturing
  3. Enterprise Resource Planning
