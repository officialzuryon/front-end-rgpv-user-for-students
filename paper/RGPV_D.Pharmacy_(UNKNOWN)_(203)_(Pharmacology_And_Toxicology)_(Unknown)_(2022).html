Final Year Pharmacy

PHARMACOLOGY AND TOXICOLOGY

(203)

Time: Three Hours Maximum Marks : 80

Note:

  1. Attempt total six questions.
  2. Question No. 1 is compulsory.
  3. From the remaining questions attempt any five.
  4. Figures to the right indicate full marks.
  5. Illustrate your answers with neat sketches wherever necessary.
1.

Answer any five of the following.

a) Define the term Prokinetics with two suitable examples.
b) Give examples of any four drugs under category Diuretics.
c) Write any four ADR of Phenytoin.
d) Define term Cardiotonics with examples of drug in this class.
e) Give common uses and dose Omeprazole.
f) Give two examples of drug in each class.
  1. Enzyme Inducer
  2. Enzyme inhibitor
5×2=10
2.

Answer any four of the following.

a) Discuss in brief note on Proton pump inhibitors.
b) Discuss pharmacology of Aspirin.
c) Classify Anticancer agents with examples of each class.
d) Give pharmacological action of Adrenaline.
e) Elaborate term Immunosuppressant.
4×3.5=14
3.

Write short note on any four.

a) Myasthenia Gravis
b) Antiulcer agents
c) Parkinson's Disease
d) Expectorants
e) Antidiarrheals
4×3.5=14
4.

Answer any two of the following.

a) Mention various routes of administration of drugs, with their advantages and disadvantages.
b) Discuss pharmacology of Morphine.
c) Discuss pharmacology of Histamine.

5.

Answer any four of the following.

a) Give a short answer on Hypoglycemic agents.
b) Discuss in short pharmacology of beta-Blockers.
c) Write a note on 5HT.
d) Elaborate term Neuromuscular blockers.
e) Write a note on treatment of Anemia.
4×3.5=14
6.

Write short note on any four.

a) Prostaglandins
b) Analeptics
c) Plasma expanders
d) Factors modifying drugs action
e) Hypolipidemic agents
4×3.5=14
7.

Answer any four of the following.

a) Give a short answer on Anti-thyroid drugs.
b) Give a brief account on penicillin.
c) Classify Antihypertensive agents with examples of each class.
d) Define and classify Purgative, with a brief note on Bulk Laxative.
e) Define epilepsy classify anticonvulsants, Give mechanism of Sodium Valproate.
