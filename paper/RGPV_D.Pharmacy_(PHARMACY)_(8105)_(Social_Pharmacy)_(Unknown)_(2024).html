First Year Pharmacy

SOCIAL PHARMACY

(ER20-15T)

Time : Three Hours Maximum Marks : 80

Note :

i) Attempt any five questions.

ii) All questions carry equal marks.

1.

Attempt any two questions.

a) What are the role of pharmacist in Public Health? Explain.
b) Write the definition of health according to WHO. What are the dimensions of health? Explain.
c) Give a detailed note on Pharmacoeconomics.
2.

Write short note on any four.

a) Balanced diet
b) Nutraceuticals
c) Drug-Food interaction
d) Effect of environment on health
e) Solid waste disposal
3.

Attempt any two.

a) Define the following terms :
  1. Pandemic
  2. Epidemic
  3. Endemic
  4. Incubation period
b) Write the difference between the following.
  1. Quarantine and Isolation
  2. Morbidity and Mortality
c) Write a brief note on water pollution. And name water-borne diseases.
4.

Attempt any two.

a) Name different contraceptive methods. Discuss in detail permanent methods.
b) What are Respiratory infections? Give a brief account on viral hepatitis.
c) Write a note on Millennium Development Goals.
5.

Attempt any two.

a) What are the ill effects of junk foods? Explain.
b) What is Noise pollution and what are the health effects of Noise?
c) Define vaccines and its types in detail.
6.

Write short note on any four.

a) FIP development goals
b) Whooping cough
c) Genetically modified foods
d) Malaria
e) Importance of breast feeding
7.

Attempt any two.

a) What are indicators of health? Explain.
b) Write a brief note on Tuberculosis.
c) Define drug abuse. And discuss its impact on social health and suicidal behaviours.
8.

Attempt any two.

a) Give an introduction to health system and name all ongoing National Health Programs.
b) What are STDs? Explain HIV/AIDS.
c) Write a detailed note on Environmental Pollution due to Pharmaceuticals.
