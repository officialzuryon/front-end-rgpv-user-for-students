First Year Pharmacy

PHARMACEUTICS

(ER20-11T)

Time : Three Hours
Maximum Marks : 80

Note : i) Attempt any five questions out of eight.

ii) Each question carry equal marks.

1.
a) How did the profession of pharmacy evolve in India. 8
b) Discuss the job opportunities of Pharmacist in India in Government sector. 4
c) Give advantage and disadvantage of glass as packaging materials. 4
2.
Describe construction, working, advantage, disadvantage and application any two of the following:
16
a) Hammer Mill
b) Cyclone Separator
c) Silverson mixer homogenizer
d) Membrane and Sintered glass filters
3.
a) Give classification of powders according to I.P. 8
b) Classify Mixtures. Give the object of mixing. 8
4.
a) Define pharmaceutical additives. Give examples of preservatives and their role in pharmaceutical products. 8
b) Give advantage and disadvantage of Tablets. 8
5.
a) Give the formula, method of preparation, storage and uses of simple syrup. 8
b) Describe the properties of an ideal ointment base. 4
c) Give a briefly classification of ointment base. 4
6.
Differentiate any four of the following:
16
a) Monophasic and Biphasic liquid
b) Wet gum and Dry gum method for emulsion
c) Creaming and Cracking of emulsion
d) Simple syrup and Elixir
e) Infusion and Decoction
7.
a) Describe advantage, disadvantage and characteristic of sterile formulation. 8
b) What are the general requirements for premises and materials according to GMP? 4
c) Discuss advantage and disadvantage of Novel Drug Delivery System. 4
8.
Write note of the following (Any four):
16
a) Microphases
b) Types of Immunity
c) Covid-19
d) Advantage of Tablet Coating
e) Disintegrants and Diluents used in tablets
