Second Year Pharmacy

HOSPITAL AND CLINICAL PHARMACY

(ER20-25T)

Time : Three Hours Maximum Marks : 80

Note :

  1. Attempt any five questions.
  2. Each question carry equal marks.
1.

Attempt any two:

a) Discuss the objectives and functions of Pharmacy and Therapeutic Committee.

b) What are the roles of Pharmacist in Preventing Antimicrobial Resistance? Explain.

c) Explain computer application in Hospital Pharmacy.

2.

Write note on any two:

a) Clinical significance of drug interaction.

b) LASA drugs and Tallman lettering as per ISMP.

c) Good Pharmacy Practice (GPP) in hospital

3.

Attempt any two:

a) Write a brief note on Radiopharmaceuticals, its storage and disposal.

b) Write definition, scope and development of Clinical Pharmacy in India

c) Discuss FEFO and FIFO methods.

4.

Attempt any two:

a) Discuss the distribution of drugs to ICCU, NICU and Emergency wards.

b) Define Poisoning and its types. Write the note on Antidotes.

c) Write definition, aim and scope of Pharmacovigilance.

5.

Write note on any four:

a) Procedure of Drug information and Poisons information.

b) Drug Basket Method.

c) Hospital Formulary

d) Total parenteral nutrition

e) Software used in hospital pharmacy

6.

Attempt any two:

a) Write definition, types, consequence and strategies to minimize medication error.

b) Discuss the significance of clinical laboratory tests used in the evaluation of disease state and name different clinical laboratory tests.

c) Write a brief note on Drugs and Poison information center and their services.

7.

Attempt any two:

a) Discuss briefly Inventory Control Technique.

b) Write a note on Automated Drug dispensing systems and devices.

c) Write a brief note on procedures of Drug Purchases.

8.

Attempt any two:

a) Write definition, types and clinical significance of drug interactions.

b) Write a note on Medication Therapy Management.

c) Discuss briefly Adverse drug reaction monitoring.
