MPH-201T

M.Pharmacy (PCI), II Semester

Examination, June 2025

Molecular Pharmaceutics

(Nano Technology and Targeted DDS)

Time : Three Hours
Maximum Marks : 70

Note: i) Attempt any five questions.

ii) All questions carry equal marks.

1.
a) Explain different types of drug targeting and write in detail about first order targeting with examples.
b) Write a note on carriers.
2.
Explain in detail about tumor targeting.
3.
a) Explain various transport mechanisms across blood brain barrier.
b) Describe in detail invasive and non-invasive brain targeting methods.
4.
Write about methods of preparation and evaluation of aerosols.
5.
a) What are liposomes? Classify liposomes and explain the structural components of liposomes.
b) Explain the preparation and evaluation of liposomes.
6.
a) Dry powder inhaler.
b) Metered dose inhalers.
7.
a) Explain about bone marrow transplantation in ex-vivo gene therapy.
b) Explain the nebulizers with suitable diagrams.
8.
Discuss the methods for the preparation of phytosomes and their evaluation.
