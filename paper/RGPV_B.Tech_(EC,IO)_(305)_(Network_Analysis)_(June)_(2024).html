1.

a) In the circuit of fig-1 the switch S is moved from position 1 to 2 at t = 0, having been in position 1 for a long time before t = 0. Capacitor C1 is uncharged at t = 0.

  2. Find the particular solution for v2(t) for t > 0

  1. t > 0 के लिए i(t) का पार्टिकुलर सोल्यूशन पता करें
  2. t > 0 के लिए v2(t) का पार्टिकुलर सोल्यूशन पता करें

b) The circuit of fig. 2 reaches a steady state in position 2 and at t = 0 the switch S is moved to position 1. Find i(t).
