SM/2025/8110 Total Pages : 4

Second Year Pharmacy

HOSPITAL AND CLINICAL PHARMACY

(ER20-25T)

Time : Three Hours Maximum Marks : 80

Note :

  1. This Paper is having three sections.
  2. All sections are compulsory.
  3. Attempt questions as per instructions given in each section.

Section-A

(Objective Type Questions)

Note : Answer all 20 questions (Multiple Choice Questions/ Fill in the Blanks / One Word / One sentence Questions). Each question carries one mark.

20 × 1 = 20

  1. What are the objectives of the Pharmacy and Therapeutics Committee?
    (a) To ensure safe and effective use of medications in the hospital
    (b) To manage the hospital pharmacy department
    (c) To develop and manage the hospital pharmacy
    (d) To purchase medications for the hospital
  2. What is the documentation required for drug purchases and inventory control?
    (a) Purchase orders and invoices
    (b) Stock cards and inventory reports
    (c) Expiration date records
    (d) All of the above
  3. What is the purpose of an automated drug dispensing system?
    (a) To reduce medication errors
    (b) To increase the workload of hospital staff
    (c) To decrease patient satisfaction
    (d) None of the above
  4. How are narcotics typically stored in a hospital pharmacy?
    (a) In the same storage area as other medications
    (b) In a separate, secured area
    (c) In the hospital's cafeteria
    (d) None of the above
  5. What are the different types of thyroid function tests?
    (a) T3, T4, and TSH tests
    (b) Liver enzyme tests
    (c) Renal function tests
    (d) Cardiac enzyme tests
  6. What is the purpose of pulmonary function tests?
    (a) To evaluate the function of the liver
    (b) To evaluate the function of the kidneys
    (c) To evaluate the function of the lungs
    (d) To evaluate the function of the heart
  7. Which of the following is an example of a pharmacy software commonly used in hospital pharmacy practice?
    (a) Microsoft Word
    (b) Adobe Photoshop
    (c) EPIC
    (d) Google Chrome
  8. What is bulk compounding?
    (a) A process of preparing medications in large quantities
    (b) A process of dispensing medications in bulk to patients
    (c) A process of manufacturing medications on a large scale
    (d) A process of administering medications to patients
  9. What is the role of a pharmacist in the emergency ward?
    (a) To prescribe medications for patients
    (b) To administer medications to patients
    (c) To prepare and dispense medications for patients
    (d) To provide counseling to patients
  10. What is the primary focus of an ICCU or ICU?
    (a) Management of chronic illnesses
    (b) Management of infectious diseases
    (c) Management of critical or life-threatening conditions
    (d) None of the above
  11. 11. Increase in heart rate is ___________.
  12. 12. The outpatient who can walk and receive primary health care is called __________.
  13. 13. ___________ international organization has developed the Good Pharmacy Practice (GPP) guidelines for hospital pharmacies?
  14. 14. Universal antidote contains __________.
  15. 15. Cold storage temperature is __________.
  16. 16. What is an ICU?
  17. 17. Which test is used to detect bile pigments?
  18. 18. What is the purpose of renal function tests?
  19. 19. Full form of an ICCU.
  20. 20. Which branch of medicine focuses on the care of infants, children, and adolescents?

Section-B

(Short Answer Type Questions)

Note : Answer any ten questions out of Eleven. Each question carries three marks.

10 × 3 = 30

  1. 1. What is the function of hospital pharmacy?
  2. 2. What is good pharmacy practice?
  3. 3. Write about infection control committee.
  4. Write short note on (any two)
    4. (a) Schedule H1 Drugs
    (b) Reversed Antibiotic
    (c) Emergency drugs
  5. 5. What is ABC analysis? Write their advantages and disadvantages.
  6. 6. Write a note on the drug distribution in hospitals.
  7. 7. Enlist the various liver function tests.
  8. 8. Classify the drug interactions and their clinical significance.
  9. 9. What is the storage of radiopharmaceuticals?
  10. 10. Define the term Pediatric, Geriatric and Postnatal care.
  11. 11. Define Pharmacovigilance and their importance.

Section-C

(Long Answer Type Questions)

Note : Answer any six questions out of seven. Each question carries five marks.

6 × 5 = 30

  1. 1. Describe the role of computers in hospital pharmacy practice.
  2. 2. Write in brief about objectives and functions of Pharmaceutical therapeutic committee.
  3. 3. What is Inventory control? Discuss the techniques of inventory control.
  4. 4. Write a note on distribution of Narcotic and Psychotropic substances.
  5. 5. Discuss the types of poisoning with their clinical conditions and antidotes with examples.
  6. 6. Explain the strategies to minimize medication errors.
  7. 7. Write a detail note on Medication therapy Management.
