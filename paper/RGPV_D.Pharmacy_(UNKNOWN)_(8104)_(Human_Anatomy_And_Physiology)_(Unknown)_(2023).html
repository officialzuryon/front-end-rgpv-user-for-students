First Year Pharmacy

HUMAN ANATOMY AND PHYSIOLOGY

(ER20-14T)

Time : Three Hours

Maximum Marks : 80

Note :

i) Attempt any five questions out of eight.

ii) All questions carry equal marks.

1.

Attempt the following: (4 each)

a) Explain lymphatic system, composition, function and its formation.

b) What is autonomic nervous system? What are the functions of sympathetic nervous system.

c) Discuss the physiology of menstruation.

d) Write the full form of the following:
RBM, ANS, TSH, GIT.

2.

Attempt the following: (4 each)

a) Write a short note on accommodation.

b) Explain in brief the various connective tissues.

c) What are endocrine glands? Name the hormones of pituitary gland and their functions.

d) Briefly describe the various types of cartilages.

3.

Attempt the following: (8 each)

a) Describe the following.
Epithelial Tissue, Connective Tissue, Muscular Tissue, Nervous Tissue.

b) What are blood groups? What is their importance in blood transfusion?

4.

Attempt the following: (8 each)

a) Write briefly about the following:
Menopause, Hypertension, Spermatogenesis.

b) Describe the urinary system and physiology of urine formation.

5.

Attempt the following: (8 each)

a) Discuss briefly cardiac cycle and write the factors affecting BP.

b) Describe in detail the various parts of the respiratory system with the help of diagram. What are its functions?

6.

Attempt the following: (4 each)

a) Differentiate between inhalation and expiration.

b) Write a note on anatomy of ear and physiology of hearing.

c) Write a note on blood pressure and its regulation.

d) Discuss physiology of muscle contraction.

7.

Attempt the following: (8 each)

a) Explain: Hemopoeisis, Anemia, Beri Beri, Tidal Volume.

b) Explain in detail physiology of respiration.

8.

Attempt the following: (8 each)

a) Discuss the physiology of Digestion or Absorption of food.

b) Explain different types of joints in the human body.
