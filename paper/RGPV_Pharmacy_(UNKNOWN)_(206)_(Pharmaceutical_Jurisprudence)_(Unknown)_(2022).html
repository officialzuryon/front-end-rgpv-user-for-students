Final Year Pharmacy

PHARMACEUTICAL JURISPRUDENCE

(206)

Time: Three Hours
Maximum Marks: 80

Note:

  1. Attempt total six questions.
  2. Question No. 1 is compulsory.
  3. From the remaining questions attempt any five.
1.
a) State true/false. (Any Five) 5×1=5
  1. Any drug which does not meet I.P. specifications is called misbranded.
  2. State pharmacy councils can inspect pharmacy colleges in their respective jurisdiction.
  3. All injections are to be supplied on the prescription of RMP.
  4. Education regulation is prepared by pharmacy council of India with approval of central Government.
  5. A bonded laboratory should necessarily have a raw materials store, finished good store and a manufacturing room.
  6. Government Analysts should have no financial interest in any business.
b) Fill in the blanks (Any five) 5×1=5
  1. Coca plant may be cultivated for the preparation of _______.
  2. The name of a pharmacist can be removed from the register if the same was entered due to _______.
  3. Patent medicines cannot be imported unless their _______ given.
  4. All opium produced should be delivered to _______.
  5. All schedule C drugs should be tested for _______.
  6. No _______ should be supplied as physician sample.
2.
Attempt any two of the following: 2×7=14
  1. Give the constitution and function of pharmacy council of India.
  2. Write the registration procedure to be "Registered Pharmacist".
  3. What type of advertisements are exempted under the drug and magic Remedies act?
3.
Attempt any two of the following: 2×7=14
  1. Explain the schedule X drugs under the drugs and cosmetic Act 1948.
  2. Write import and export of narcotics and psychotropics substances as per Indian act.
  3. How many types of licenses can be issued for sale of drugs and cosmetics? Give brief account of each of them.
4.
Attempt any two of the following: 2×7=14
  1. What are the qualification for appointment as Government Analyst? Explain his duties.
  2. Define drugs, spurious drugs and misbranded drugs.
  3. Discuss the qualification and duties of drug inspector.
5.
Attempt any two of the following: 2×7=14
  1. Define medicinal and Toilet preparation. Discuss the procedure for obtaining the spirit in bonded laboratory.
  2. Write procedure for import of drugs under drugs and cosmetics act 1948.
  3. Who can terminate a pregnancy? What experience/training should such person posses?
6.
Attempt any two of the following: 2×7=14
  1. Explain the "Pharmacist Oath". How can a pharmacist fulfill it.
  2. Discuss the labelling and packing requirement of ophthalmic preparation.
  3. What is the object of Poison act? How are the possession and sale of Poisons regulated under the Act?
7.
Attempt any two of the following: 2×7=14
  1. DTAB
  2. Drug Price Control Order (latest)
  3. Patent and proprietary drugs
