1. a)

Case Study: Notre Dame Cathedral Fire.

On 15 April 2019, around 18:20 CEST, a structural fire broke out in the roof space of Notre-Dame de Paris, a medieval Catholic cathedral in Paris, France. Discuss the problems encountered during the rescue operation.

केस स्टडी: नोट्रे डेम कैथेड्रल आग घटना।

15 अप्रैल 2019 को, लगभग 18:20 CEST, फ्रांस के पेरिस में एक मध्ययुगीन कैथोलिक कैथेड्रल, नोट्रे-डेम डे पेरिस की छत पर एक संरचनात्मक आग लग गई। रेस्क्यू ऑपरेशन के दौरान आने वाली समस्याओं पर चर्चा करें।

b)

Case Study: Fukushima Daiichi Nuclear Accident.

The Fukushima nuclear accident was a major nuclear accident at the Fukushima Daiichi nuclear power plant in Okuma, Fukushima, Japan which began on March 11, 2011. The proximate cause of the accident was the 2011 Tohoku earthquake and tsunami, which resulted in electrical grid failure and damaged nearly all of the power plant's backup energy sources. The subsequent inability to sufficiently cool reactors after shutdown compromised containment and resulted in the release of radioactive contaminants into the surrounding environment. Explain the rescue techniques during the disaster in nuclear plant.

केस स्टडी: फुकुशिमा दाइची परमाणु दुर्घटना।
