Quantitative Techniques In Management

Time : Three Hours Maximum Marks : 70

Note : i) Attempt any five questions.

ii) All questions carry equal marks.

1. a)
Convert the following LPP into standard form
maximize u = 2x + 6y + 5z'
Subject to the conditions
5x – 2y + 3z ≤ 9, x + y + z ≥ 5; x + 2y = 7
and the non negativity conditions x ≥ 0, y ≥ 0, z ≥ 0.
b)
Differentiate between general LPP and standard LPP.
2. a)
Solve the following LPP by resolving degeneracy:
maximize z = 5x + 3y
x + y ≤ 2,
5x + 2y ≤ 10
53x + 8y ≤ 12
and the non negativity conditions x ≥ 0, y ≥ 0, z ≥ 0.
b)
Explain the method of dual simplex algorithm. What are the essential difference between simplex method and dual simplex method.
