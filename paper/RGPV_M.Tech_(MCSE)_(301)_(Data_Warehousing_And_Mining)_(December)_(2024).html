MCSE-301(A)
M.E./M.Tech., III Semester
Examination, December 2024
Data Warehousing and Mining
(Elective - I)
Time : Three Hours
Maximum Marks: 70
Note: i) Attempt any five questions.
ii) All questions carry equal marks.
1.
What is Data Mining? Draw and explain the architecture of Data Mining System.
2.
Describe the steps involved in data mining when viewed as a process of knowledge discovery.
3.
What is Association Rule Mining? Explain the A priori algorithm with a suitable example.
4.
What is categorical clustering algorithm? Explain anyone algorithm with example.
5.
Describe neural network. How the neural network is useful in Data Mining?
6.
Compare and contrast the use of Fuzzy Logic, and Genetic Algorithms in terms of their suitability for different types of web mining tasks.
7.
Write Short Notes upon
i)
Web Mining
ii)
Spatial Mining
8.
Describe the Image and Video representation techniques in Data Mining.
