Computer Aided Drug Delivery System

Course: M.Pharmacy Code: 203T Branch: MPH Date: June 2025

[Total No. of Printed Pages: 2

Roll No.

MPH-203T

M.Pharmacy (PCI), II Semester

Examination, June 2025

Computer Aided Drug Delivery System

Time: Three Hours

Maximum Marks: 70

Note: i) Attempt any five questions.

ii) All questions carry equal marks.

1. a)

Describe about the ethics of computing in pharmaceutical research.

b)

Explain the role of use of computer and the internet in scholarly information retrieval.

2. a)

Explain about the confidence region.

b)

Write the advantage, principle, stakeholders of QbD.

3. a)

Write the concept, benefits & parameters of optimization.

b)

Brief the ICH Q8, Q9 and Q10 guidelines.

4. a)

Write the computer simulation of isolated tissue & organs.

b)

Discuss about e - clinic software in detail.

5. a)

What are the major elements for the successful prediction of drug metabolism.

b)

Outline the steps involved in constructing a gastrointestinal absorption simulation model. What data and information are required for building an accurate model?

6. a)

Define a virtual trial in the context of biopharmaceutical characterization. How does it differ from traditional clinical trials?

b)

Write the various steps involved in the design of experiments.

7. a)

Define artificial intelligence and write the application of AI in pharmaceutical drug development.

b)

Discuss about any one robotic applications in the pharmaceutical industry.

8.

Write short note on any two :-

a)

CFD application in freeze drying

b)

Biowaiver considerations

c)

Expert system used in QSAR to predict toxicology

d)

Type of data used in statistics
