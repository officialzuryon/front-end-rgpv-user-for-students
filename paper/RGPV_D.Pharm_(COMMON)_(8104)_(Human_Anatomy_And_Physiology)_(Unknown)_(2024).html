First Year Pharmacy

HUMAN ANATOMY AND PHYSIOLOGY

(ER20-14T)

Time : Three Hours
Maximum Marks : 80

Note :

  1. Attempt any five questions.
  2. All questions carry equal marks.
  3. Draw neat and labelled diagram wherever necessary.
1.

Attempt any two.

a) Briefly describe the physiology of digestion.
b) Discuss the physiology of urine formation.
c) Differentiate between CNS and ANS.
2.

Attempt any two.

a) Enlist different epithelial tissue.
b) Draw a labelled diagram of respiratory system. Discuss the chemical factors controlling respiration.
c) Write a short note on :
  1. ECG
  2. Hypertension
3.

Explain (any four)

a) Cardiac cycle
b) Composition of blood
c) Tidal volume
d) Micturition
e) Phagocytosis
4.

Attempt any two.

a) Give the functions of Mitochondria and Golgi bodies.
b) Draw a neat labelled diagram of internal ear.
c) Draw a neat labelled diagram of Human Brain.
5.

Attempt any two.

a) Draw a well labelled diagram of female reproductive system.
b) Draw a well labelled diagram of skin.
c) Explain 'Rh' factor.
6.

Write short note on (any four)

a) Pituitary gland
b) Pancreas
c) Adrenal gland
d) Thyroid gland
e) Thrombosis
7.

Attempt any two.

a) Discuss any two abnormal constituents of Urine.
b) Differentiate between cardiac muscle and skeletal muscle.
c) Write the functions of Medulla Oblongata and Hypothalamus.
8.

Attempt any two.

a) Classify joints with examples mention the functions of joints.
b) Explain the physiology of taste.
c) Write the functions and regulation of insulin.
