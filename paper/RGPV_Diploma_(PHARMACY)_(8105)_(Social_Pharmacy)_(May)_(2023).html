First Year Pharmacy

SOCIAL PHARMACY

(ER20-15T)

Time : Three Hours
Maximum Marks : 80

Note :

  1. Attempt any five questions.
  2. Each question carry equal marks.

1. Attempt the following:

4 each

a)
What are pesticides? Give examples. Write about various harmful effects of pesticides.
b)
What are vaccines? Classify them.
c)
Explain food safety and adulteration in foods.
d)
Enumerate various types of disasters. List the various steps of disaster management.

2. Attempt the following:

4 each

a)
Write a note on Tuberculosis.
b)
Write the causes, spread, prevention and treatment of AIDs.
c)
Explain Pulse Polio Programme in brief.
d)
Write note on Pharmacoeconomics.

3. Attempt the following:

8 each

a)
Write various environmental factors that affect health. Write a note on water and note on pollution.
b)
Define the term family planning and discuss the natural methods of contraception.

4. Attempt the following:

8 each

a)
Describe in detail solid waste disposal and control.
b)
Discuss the various indicators of health.

5. Attempt the following:

8 each

a)
Describe National Health Policy.
b)
Discuss about management of Diabetes.

6. Attempt the following:

4 each

a)
Write the ill effects of junk food.
b)
Write note on COVID-19.
c)
What is diabetes? Define Glycemic index.
d)
Write a detailed note on syphilis.

7. Attempt the following:

8 each

a)
Define drug abuse. Discuss about its impact on society.
b)
What is importance of vitamins in our diet? Classify them and give a detailed account of fat soluble vitamins.

8. Attempt the following:

8 each

a)
Write a note on dietary recommendation on high B.P. and hyperlipidemia.
b)
What are the problems of over population? How the population can be controlled? Discuss briefly natural methods of birth control.
