Grading System (GS)
English for Communication
Time: Three Hours Maximum Marks : 70

Note:

  1. Attempt any five questions.
  2. All questions carry equal marks.
1. a)
Determine whether each of the following statements is imperative, declarative, interrogative or exclamatory. 7
  1. Let’s walk together.
  2. What can one person do?
  3. We have made progress.
  4. How utterly we have failed our children!
  5. What a big challenge it is!
  6. Kill not your children because of poverty.
  7. Do you know your multiplication tables?
b)
As instructed in the brackets, respond to the following questions: 7
  1. The box of chocolates ________ (was/were) left on the kitchen counter.
  2. We went to ________ Concert last night. (a, an, the)
  3. Mathematics ________ (is/are) not my favorite subject in school.
  4. The Team ________ (is/are) practicing hard for the upcoming match.
  5. Nobody (knows, knowns) the trouble I've seen.
  6. The committee (debates, debate) these questions careful.
  7. The scientist looked ________ the microscope. (through/in)
