MMTP-302(B)

M.E./M.Tech., III Semester

Examination, November 2023

Non Conventional Energy Sources

(Elective-II)

Time: Three Hours

Maximum Marks: 70

Note:

  1. Attempt any five questions.
  2. All questions carry equal marks.
1. a)
Define energy source and how it is significant in generation of power?
b)
Write in detail about possible alternative energy sources and explain them each.
2. a)
Explain various types of commercial conventional energy sources, with possible figures.
b)
Derive estimation time 'Te' for which conventional source, that losses alternate energy?
3. a)
Write the effects of thermal storage and explain them in detail.
b)
Give in detail about the features of photo-thermal applications.
4. a)
What do you mean by energy conversion and explain why and how it is advantageous?
b)
Describe in detail about elementary design principles and how it effects the system?
5. a)
Define the concept of energy plantation and explain how it is important in generation?
b)
Explain the phenomenon of photo-voltaic conversion in real time applications.
6. a)
Discuss the process of fuel production from animal waste and explain them.
b)
Write about the steps involved in Bio-conversion in full length with suitable sketches.
7. a)
How the geothermal systems are meritorious and explain their features?
b)
What is a fuel cell, give classifications and discuss them in detail?
8.
Write a short note on any two:
  1. Photonic Energy
  2. Non-conventional Energy
  3. Growth of micro-organisms
