First Year Pharmacy

HUMAN ANATOMY AND PHYSIOLOGY

(ER20-14T)

Time: Three Hours

Maximum Marks: 80

Note :

  1. Attempt any five questions.
  2. All questions carry equal marks.
  3. Draw neat and labelled diagram wherever necessary.
1.

Attempt any two. 2×8=16

a) Explain various types of joints in Human body.

b) Explain the anatomy of Respiratory organ

c) Discuss the disorders of skeletal muscle

2.

Attempt any two. 2×8=16

a) Explain the classification of nervous system

b) Write a short note on Reflex action

c) Briefly describe the process of Oogenesis

3.

Explain any four 4×4=16

a) Various layers of human skin

b) Types of epithelia cells

c) Function of Cytoplasm and Nucleoplasm

d) EEG

e) Functions of Platelets

4.

Attempt any two. 2×8=16

a) Draw a labelled diagram of Male reproductive organ

b) Explain the physiology of menstruation

c) Explain Functions of Adrenal gland

5.

Attempt any two. 2×8=16

a) Explain in detail about Cardiac cycle

b) Draw a well labelled diagram of Cell

c) Describe the Functions of Lymph Nodes

6.

Attempt any two. 2×8=16

a) Write a short note on Medulla oblongata

b) Briefly describes Blood pressure and its regulation

c) Describe various types of cranial nerves

7.

Write short note on (any four) 4×4=16

a) Parathyroid gland

b) Function of Spinal cord

c) Clearance tests of Urinary system

d) Types of Nervous tissue

e) Functions of WBCs

8.

Attempt any two. 2×8=16

a) Write a short note on Pregnancy and Parturitions

b) Draw a well labelled diagram of Human urinary system

c) Differentiate between Sympathetic and Parasympathetic nervous system
