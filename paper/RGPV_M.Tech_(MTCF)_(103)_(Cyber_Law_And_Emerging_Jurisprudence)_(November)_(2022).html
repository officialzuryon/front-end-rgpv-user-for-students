Cyber Law And Emerging Jurisprudence

Course: M.Tech Code: 103 Branch: MTCF Date: November 2022

Roll No

MTCF-103

M.E./M.Tech. I Semester

Examination, November 2022

Cyber Law and Emerging Jurisprudence

Time : Three Hours

Maximum Marks : 70

Note:

  1. Attempt any five questions.
  2. All questions carry equal marks.
1. a)

What do you understand by cyber-space? Explain the jurisdiction in cyber space.

b)

What is IT act 2000? Explain role of IT Act in Cyber security.

2. a)

What do you mean by electronic contracts? What are the different types of electronic contracts? Discuss

b)

Explain concept of Jurisdictional and its type.

3. a)

Justify the faith in cyber world? Explain pros and cons of cyber world.

b)

What do you understand by Indian legal position explain in brief?

4. a)

Define the term electronic governance. And explain the term and condition for electronics Governance.

b)

Explain in detail the public key functioning and Protection provided by it under the Digital signature.

5. a)

Explain justice dispensation system for Cyber crime.

b)

Examine the method of dealing with cyber-squatting of domain names and the resulting disputes under the Trade Marks Act 1999.

6. a)

Define the term "online contract" Explain the term and conditions required for making online contracts.

b)

What do you understand by legal services in India? Discuss any one landmark case of India with detail judgment.

7. a)

Classify cyber crime and categorize cyber criminals.

b)

Define the term Hacking and Explain its essentials.

8.

Write short note on-(ANY TWO)

a)

Evidence Act.

b)

Obscenity and pornography.

c)

Internet governance.
