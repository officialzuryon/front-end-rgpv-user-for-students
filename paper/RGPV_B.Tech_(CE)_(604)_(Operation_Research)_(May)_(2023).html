A television repairman finds that the time spent on his jobs has exponential distribution with a mean of 30 minutes. If he repairs the sets in the order in which they came in, and if the arrival of sets follows a Poisson distribution with an approximate average rate of 10 per 8-hour day, what is repairman expected idle time per day?
