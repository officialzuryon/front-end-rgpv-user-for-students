1.

A small manufacturer employs 15 skilled men and 30 semi-skilled men and makes an article in two qualities, a deluxe model and an ordinary model. The making of a deluxe model requires 2 hours work by a skilled man and 4 hours work by a semi skilled man. The ordinary model requires 1 hour work by a skilled man and 5 hours work by a semi skilled man. By union rules no man can work more than 8 hours per day. The manufacturer's clear profit of the deluxe model is Rs. 20 and of the ordinary model Rs. 10. Formulate the model and of the problem. 14
