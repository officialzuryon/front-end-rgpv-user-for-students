Scheme July 2011

FUNCTIONAL COMMUNICATION - I

Time : Three Hours Maximum Marks : 60

Note :

  1. All questions are compulsory.
  2. Read the instructions carefully and follow them faithfully.
  3. Do not exceed the prescribed time-limit.
  4. Instructions for the examiner: This question paper must be handed over to students after the completion of oral test.

(Practical part) (Oral Test)

1.
Activity 1 [Role-play] 5 minutes. 10

The students may be asked to play the role of (any one)

  1. Greeting a customer / guest as a hotel.
  2. Playing the role of a guide describing a historical structure in front of tourists.
2.
Activity 2 [speaking] 3 minutes. 10
  1. Self-introduction of students
  2. Students may be asked to read any article from the news-paper or a portion from their text-book.
3.
Activity 3 [Listening] 5 minutes 10

An article from the news paper or a magazine can be read to the students and simple questions can be asked to test their listening skills.
