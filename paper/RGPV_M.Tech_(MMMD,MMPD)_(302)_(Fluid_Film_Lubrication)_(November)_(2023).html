3.

The Rayleigh step bearing of length 213 mm and width 860 mm is required to support 150 kN load. The sum of surface roughness on contacting surfaces of bearing is 5 microns. The minimum oil-film thickness required is 20 times the sum of surface roughness on contacting surfaces. The sliding velocity is 8 m/s. Using optimum conditions, calculate: 14

  1. The location of step
  2. The maximum oil-film thickness
  3. The viscosity of lubricating oil
  4. The maximum pressure
  5. The ratio of maximum pressure to average pressure
