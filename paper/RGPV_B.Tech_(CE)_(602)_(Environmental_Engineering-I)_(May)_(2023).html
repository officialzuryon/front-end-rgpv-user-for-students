A water supply scheme has to be designed for a city having a population of 1,20,000. Estimate the total draft (in MLD) which is required for an average water consumption of 220 lpcd. Use Kuichling's method to calculate fire demand? 7

Calculate the velocity of flow and corresponding discharge in a sewer of circular section having diameter equal to 1 m laid at a gradient of 1 in 500. The sewer runs at a depth of 0.6 m. Use Manning's formula taking n= 0.012? 7

  • DO of aerated water = 3mg/lit

  • DO of waste water sample =0.6 mg/lit.
