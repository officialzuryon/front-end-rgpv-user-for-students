Final Year Pharmacy

PHARMACEUTICAL CHEMISTRY-II

(202)

Time: Three Hours Maximum Marks: 80

Note: i) Attempt any six questions.
ii) Question No. 1 is compulsory.

1.

Attempt any five questions: 5×2=10

a) Give the chemical structure of followings.

  1. Benzimidazole
  2. Isoxazole

b) Give the chemical name of followings.

  1. OHC –

c) Write the pharmaceutical preparation of followings.

  1. Glyceryl Trinitrate
  2. Pilocarpine

d). Give storage conditions of following.

  1. Insulin
  2. Phenol

e) Explain terms:

  1. Antiamoebic Agent
  2. Cardiotonic

f). Write down the category of followings:

  1. Diphenhydramine
  2. Salbutamol
2.

Define diuretics classify them with suitable examples. 14

3.

Write brief note on followings. (Any two) 2×7=14

a) Sympathetic Agents

b) Tranquillizers

c) Fat soluble vitamins

4.

Define the term cancer discuss Antineoplastic agents in detail. 14

5.

Write down chemical structure, name, properties and pharmaceutical compounds of followings. (Any two) 2×7=14

i) Dapsone

ii) Atropine

iii) Ethyl Nitrite

6.

What are sulphonamides? Discuss the chemistry of sulphonamides with special reference of sulphadiazine. 14

7.

Write note on any two: 2×7=14

i) Antitubercular Agents

ii) Analgesics and Antipyretics Agents

iii) Antifungal Agents

8.

Write about 1 drug of each category: (Any two) 2×7=14

i) General Anesthetic Agents

ii) Antidepressant Drugs

iii) Oral Anticoagulants
