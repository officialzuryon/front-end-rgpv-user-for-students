ℹ️ About RGPV Papers

A student-first platform dedicated to making exam preparation accessible for all

Our Mission

Empowering Every RGPV Student

RGPV Papers was built by students, for students. We noticed that finding previous year question papers was frustratingly difficult — scattered across unreliable sources or hidden behind paywalls.

We changed that. Our platform gives every RGPV and MP University student free, instant access to organized, searchable question papers — so you can focus on preparing, not searching.
