❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What are key topics for RGPV Software Engineering exam? +

SDLC models (Waterfall, Agile, Spiral), requirement engineering, UML diagrams, testing strategies (black box, white box), and project estimation (COCOMO) are most important.

Is Software Engineering a theory-heavy paper? +

Yes, it is primarily theory-based. Focus on diagram-based answers (DFD, UML, ER diagrams) and comparison questions (Waterfall vs Agile) for better marks.

About RGPV Software Engineering (CS-601) Question Papers

RGPV Software Engineering is a core subject offered in Semester 6 for Computer Science students at Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV), Bhopal. The paper code CS-601 covers fundamental concepts that are essential for building a strong foundation in software engineering.
